Have Questions? Ask Us.

AssistHer is a platform for girls to learn more about the world and womanhood while being supported by other women who have experience and different perspectives on issues.

We are a community of big sisters from all around the world.

Our purpose is to educate girls about different perspectives and techniques through which they can become more aware of themselves and decide who they are through multi-faceted advice instead of cultural/peer pressure.
